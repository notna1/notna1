Borregaard operates one of the world's most advanced and sustainable biorefineries.

Using natural, renewable raw materials, we produce advanced and environmentally friendly biochemicals that can replace oil-based products.

Utilising the different components of wood , we produce biopolymers, speciality cellulose, biovanillin, cellulose fibrils and bioethanol or a variety of applications in sectors such as agriculture and aquaculture, construction, pharmaceuticals and cosmetics, foodstuffs, batteries and biofuels.
